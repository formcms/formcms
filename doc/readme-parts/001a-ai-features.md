## Unique AI-Native Features: The Future of Web Development

FormCMS stands apart as the first **AI-native CMS**, fundamentally changing how developers and content creators interact with data and design. By integrating advanced Large Language Models (LLMs) through **Formmate**, FormCMS automates the most time-consuming aspects of building and managing web applications.

Forget manual table creation and relationship mapping. With FormCMS, you can describe your business domain in natural language (e.g., *"Design an online bookstore with authors, genres, and customer reviews"*), and the AI will:
- **Design Entities**: automatically create the necessary database tables.
- **Define Attributes**: choose appropriate data types (String, Int, Datetime, etc.) and display types (Image, Editor, Lookup).
- **Map Relationships**: correctly setup Many-to-One, One-to-Many, and Many-to-Many links.
